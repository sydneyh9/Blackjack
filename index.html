Welcome to Blackjack!

Objective:

Beat the dealer by getting a hand as close to 21 as possible, without going over.

Rules:

• If both dealer and player go over 21, the better hand wins.

• You may only draw one card per turn.

• Only one deck of 52 is used.

• The dealer's first card is hidden.

• The Ace may be used as either 1 or 11 as per the dealer or player's needs.

  • Click Deal to start a new game.
  • Click Stay to end your turn and let the dealer play.
  • Keep a close eye on your score!
